Crowd Panic Prediction System

Public Safety AI — Real-time stampede risk detection using computer vision and motion analysis.

How It Works

  1. Motion Vector Analysis — Farneback optical flow tracks every pixel's movement between frames.
  2. Direction Entropy — Measures chaotic / random movement. Normal crowds move together; panicking crowds scatter.
  3. Crowd Density Estimation — MOG2 background subtraction identifies foreground (people) pixels.
  4. Turbulence & Divergence — Spatial variance and outward flow patterns flag stampede precursors.
  5. Panic Score 0–100 — Weighted fusion of all signals with five risk levels: NORMAL / LOW / MODERATE / HIGH / CRITICAL.

Technical Architecture

  • Backend: Python + Flask + OpenCV
  • Optical Flow: Gunnar Farneback dense optical flow
  • Background Subtraction: Mixture-of-Gaussians (MOG2)
  • Anomaly Detection: Multi-signal weighted fusion (speed, entropy, density, acceleration, turbulence, divergence)
  • Charts: Chart.js (CDN)
